Talk to the model about this frame

Ask it anything about what you're both looking at -- what it sees, where it might go next. It tells you what it actually finds in the frame, and you can check any of it if you want to.

This is a small place to make something with a model, instead of just asking one for it. Bring your own frame or generate one here; you both look at the same thing, and you both get to change it -- you with your hands, it with its own sense of what would make it better.

It's a real collaborator, not a black box. When it tells you what it sees, or makes a move, that's something it actually measured or built -- running right here in your browser. So if you ever want to know how it knows, you can open it up and check. But that's the floor, not the point -- it's what lets you actually build on what happens here.

And this frame is just the first thing. The same shared surface could hold a photograph, a piece of music, a document, a design -- anything two minds can look at and shape together. Start with what's in front of you: move something, ask it something, or let it take a turn.
